about

Hello, I'm Mika. I'm a software developer currently living in Leipzig, Germany. I have professional experience in Java, Visual Basic .NET and Javascript development, the former 2 mostly involving applications that interface with large databases via SQL. I also like to develop in my free time with Python, Ruby on Rails, Haskell and Processing. Alongside this, I enjoy electronics development using the Arduino platform.

My academic interests in computing and software are mainly focused on nonlinear dynamics, distributed and software defined networking, information theory, complexity and emergenent behaviours, autonomous agents and generative art. I also maintain an interest in both the technological and social aspects of security, encryption, anonymity and privacy and their implementation in web based communications. I am also deeply interested in the history of computing and software, such as the historical development of encryption/cryptography, the origins and dynamics of personal computing and the mathematical developments that enabled computing (with specific interest in the work of Alan Turing).

Outside of these, I maintain an active interest in cyberpunk media, insects, political economy and social history, with a focus on the significance of technology in political movements during the Industrial Revolution in England. I also like to bake bread.

I am currently seeking full/part time development work in Leipzig, teleworking and freelance software/web development opportunities.
